Ballads 1

"Ballads 1" is the debut studio album by Japanese singer-songwriter Joji. It serves as a follow-up to In Tongues EP, his first project as Joji, and Pink Season, his only album released under the now-retired alter-ego Pink Guy. It was released on 26 October 2018 through 88rising and 12Tone Music. It features the singles "Yeah Right", "Slow Dancing In The Dark", "Can't Get Over You" and "Test Drive".

The following track,"Yeah Right",is the lead single of the album.
